# Unified WOMAC-KOOS Knee Assessment Questionnaire

## Overview
This unified questionnaire contains ALL questions from both the WOMAC and KOOS instruments. By completing this single form, both WOMAC and KOOS scores can be calculated separately.

**Administration Details:**
- **Total Items:** 42 questions (same as KOOS)
- **Estimated Time:** 10-12 minutes
- **Scoring:** Both WOMAC and KOOS scores derivable
- **Time Frame:** All questions refer to the **LAST WEEK**

## Instructions
This survey asks for your view about your knee. This information will help us keep track of how you feel about your knee and how well you are able to perform your usual activities.

**Answer every question by marking ONE box for each question.** If you are unsure about how to answer a question, please give the best answer you can.

**All questions refer to your knee symptoms and function during the LAST WEEK.**
